TradeVision Pro - Software Requirements & Project Blueprint
Project Type: 
Professional TradingView-Inspired Trading Analysis & Paper Trading Platform
Technology Stack: 
Java, JavaFX, MySQL, JDBC, REST APIs, MVC Architecture
Project Level: 
Advanced SDA / Portfolio / Final Year Project Foundation
1. Problem Statement
Most beginner traders lack access to professional market analysis platforms. Existing platforms are often expensive or difficult to understand. The goal of TradeVision Pro is to provide a professional trading analysis platform with advanced charting, indicators, paper trading, strategy testing, alerts, and portfolio management.
2. Project Objectives
Build a TradingView-inspired desktop application.
Provide interactive market charts.
Implement paper trading without real money.
Provide technical indicators and drawing tools.
Allow users to test strategies on historical data.
Generate performance analytics and trading reports.
3. User Roles
Trader:
- Analyze markets
- Create watchlists
- Place paper trades
- Test strategies
- Manage portfolio
Administrator:
- Manage users
- Monitor system activity
- View reports
- Manage market assets and alerts
